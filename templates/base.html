NBP App

Displays information about various currencies and gold prices

Currency Exchange Rates

This app provides the exchange rates for major currencies.

To access exchange rate information for a specific currency, the app fetches real-time data from the National Bank of Poland (NBP).

Gold Prices

The app also provides the latest gold prices, directly sourced from the NBP API.

Features:

Get real-time exchange rates for popular currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, etc.).

Access gold prices based on the latest data from the National Bank of Poland.

Easy-to-use interface to fetch currency rates and gold prices with just a few clicks.

Usage Instructions

Simply select a currency from the list, enter the desired amount, and the app will calculate the exchange rate in PLN (Polish Zloty).

To view the gold prices, simply navigate to the gold price section where the latest prices will be shown.

How It Works

Select the desired currency from a dropdown list.

Enter the amount you want to convert to PLN.

The app calculates the conversion and shows you the result.

The data for exchange rates and gold prices is fetched from the NBP API.
